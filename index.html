WHAT IS DR. AIR?

Future

HELICOPTERS

In the past, planes were not conceived as a method of transportation, nor a a means of scientific discovery.

But not because they didn't want, but because the industrial advances were not like now; although the left an open path for us.

n the past, basic knowledge evolved in an amazing way with the transcurrence of the two world wars, in which aviation had its greatest stage of development.

Important characteristics at that time were:

  • Wood and fabric wings.
  • Not aerodynamical bases.
  • Less powerfull propellers.
  • They discover the initial mechanics of flying.
